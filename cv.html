Personal Letter

Hello! My name is Jumi Strandberg.

I'm a curious person who grew up in the lovely city of Malmö. In my dear hometown, I completed my international primary and secondary education, and I speak both English and Swedish as my first languages.

During my high school years, I became interested in languages and culture and volunteered to teach English to immigrants in my spare time. I further pursued this interest when I worked as a private tutor in Korea for a couple of years, during which I also polished my Korean language skills.

I left Korea to study full-stack web development and international business management as a double major at an international school in Thailand. I took on various roles during my education, working on real projects, including one for Bangkok's traffic authority. Now, I'm back in Sweden, ready for new challenges!

I have a wide range of interests, and my secret superpower is finding charm in everything and everyone. My motto has always been, "everyone does their best," and I genuinely believe in it. Additionally, I enjoy working out, savoring delicious vegan food, painting, and learning new languages.

I look forward to getting to know you!
Hugs,
